BACKGROUND

All first year MS-HCI students at Georgia Tech are required to take “HCI Research Methods”, where they split into 4-person teams, research a given problem, and develop a solution over the course of their first semester.

PROBLEM

Vegans and vegetarians often struggle to maintain a well-balanced and diverse diet, especially when dining out. They also struggle with finding vegetarian and vegan options in restaurants that aren’t dedicated to serving vegetarian and vegan food.

SOLUTION

On-the-Fly View
  • See what vegan and vegetarian options are around you
  • Check out pictures of menu items
  • Filter to fit a specific dietary restriction
Restaurant View
  • Learn more about a specific restaurant
  • Check out menu items that comply with a dietary restriction
  • Get directions to the restaurant or order online
History View
  • Travel back in time to see restaurants you’ve passed by
  • View menu items and friendliness towards a dietary restriction
