FitTogether

Work out with friends, no matter how far.

  • Journey Mapping
  • Affinity Diagramming
TOOLS I USED
  • Figma
  • Microsoft Teams

BACKGROUND

All first year MS-HCI students at Georgia Tech are required to take “Foundations of Human-Computer Interaction”, where they split into 4-person teams, identify a problem space, and develop a solution over the course of their first semester.

PROBLEM

With COVID-19 forcing people apart from their loved ones, two problems that we kept encountering were keeping in touch with friends over the internet, and staying in shape while isolated. A few of us relied on workout buddies, and without that social accountability, were struggling to maintain motivation.

SOLUTION

Jump In View
  • Spontaneously join your friends while exercising
  • Ping your friends to exercise with you
  • Join a scheduled workout session
Tutorials View
  • Find online videos to suit your exercise needs
  • Get recommendations based on prior workouts
  • Save videos for later workouts
Live Workout View
  • See your friends exercising with you
  • Control the workout video in sync
  • Toggle your microphone and camera when life gets in the way
